Welcome!

The 11th Mid-Atlantic Student Colloquium on Speech, Language and Learning (MASC-SLL 2024) is a one day event bringing together students, postdocs, faculty and researchers from universities and industry in the Mid-Atlantic area. The colloquium is an opportunity for students and postdocs to present preliminary or completed work and to network with other researchers working in related fields.

Important Dates

  • Submission opens: March 22
  • Submission deadline: April 8 April 15
  • Decisions announced: April 15
  • Registration opens: April 15
  • Registration closes: April 26
  • Colloquium: May 3

All deadlines are at 11:59pm EST (GMT-5).
