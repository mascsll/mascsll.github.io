• Location: the event takes place at Science Education and Research Center (SERC), 1925 N 12th Street, Philadelphia.
    • Room 116 (9am-11am and 2pm-6pm) - go left after entering SERC building, room is on the left side of the hall
    • Lobby (11am - 2pm) - go right after entering SERC building
  • Parking:
    • You can park on street around SERC (Montgomery, 11th, 12th, 13th, Cecil B. Moore, Norris) - there should be enough space as it is Saturday. Make sure that parking is allowed!
    • The nearest parking garage is Montgomery Garage ($4/hour), 1859 N 11th Street, Philadelphia.
    • You can also use Tuttleman Lot ($6/hour), 1205 W Montgomery Ave.
    • If you decide to park in a garage, we suggest buying a Weekend Parking Pass ($30). See more details about visitor parking spots here.
