Welcome!

The 13th Mid-Atlantic Student Colloquium on AI, Language and Learning (MASC-ALL 2026) is a one day event bringing together students, postdocs, faculty and researchers from universities and industry in the Mid-Atlantic area. The colloquium is an opportunity for students and postdocs to present preliminary or completed work and to network with other researchers working in related fields.

The 13th Mid-Atlantic Student Colloquium on AI, Language, and Learning (MASC-ALL 2026) will be held during Research Month at Howard University (IRB Building - Howard University Interdisciplinary Research Building (IRB), 2201 Georgia Avenue NW, Washington, DC 20059) on April 25, 2026 Check the "Directions" page to find your way to us!
