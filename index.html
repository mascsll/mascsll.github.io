Welcome!

The 8th Mid-Atlantic Student Colloquium on Speech, Language and Learning (MASC-SLL 2020) is a one day event bringing together students, postdocs, faculty and researchers from universities and industry in the Mid-Atlantic area. The colloquium is an opportunity for students and postdocs to present preliminary or completed work and to network with other researchers working in related fields.

This year MASC-SLL will be held at the University of Maryland, College Park (UMD), on Friday, March 6th, 2020. There is no registration charge and refreshments will be provided.

Students and postdocs should submit a one page abstract describing ongoing, planned, or completed research on computational approaches to speech, language, and learning. Accepted submissions will be presented as either posters or talks. We strongly encourage you to submit anything from work-in-progress to work that has been previously published and presented at a conference. All are welcome!

Submit online at https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=mascsll2020.

Important Dates

  • Submission opens: January 27
  • Submission deadline (abstracts): February 7 February 14
  • Decisions announced: February 24
  • Registration opens: February 7 February 17
  • Registration closes: March 2
  • Colloquium: March 6

All deadlines are at 11:59pm EST (GMT-5).
